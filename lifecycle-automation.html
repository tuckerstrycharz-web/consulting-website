Two workflows.
Year one.

No existing infrastructure, no prior playbook. Every trigger condition, tag lifecycle, messaging sequence, and promo code was designed from scratch — and deployed across three services simultaneously.

Why it works: no blanket discounting
Promo codes are withheld in the first outreach phase for both workflows. Deals are only introduced after a non-converting touchpoint — saving margin on customers who would have converted anyway.
Why it works: calculated targeting
Every trigger uses a dual condition — inactivity window AND zero upcoming reservations. This eliminates false positives on customers who are already engaged, keeping list quality high.
Why it works: qualified lead handoff
Staff never call cold leads. By the time the center touches a customer, the system has already made two automated touchpoints. Every call is pre-warmed and pre-qualified.
Lead Qualification

The system qualifies. The center closes.

Both workflows handle the heavy lifting — initial outreach, list management, promo delivery. Centers receive pre-sorted, pre-warmed leads and only need to do three things.
